🏥 Clinical Note Summarizer + ICD-10 Suggestion App

A biomedical NLP pipeline that takes a doctor's free-text clinical note and returns a concise abstractive summary and ranked ICD-10 diagnosis code suggestions — powered by transformer models from HuggingFace.


Built as part of my BSc in AI with Biomedical Engineering at FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg.
⚠️ For educational purposes only. Not intended for clinical decision-making.

🔍 What it does

Paste any free-text clinical note and get back:


Abstractive summary generated by DistilBART-CNN transformer
Top-3 ICD-10 category suggestions with confidence scores via zero-shot classification (BART-large-MNLI)


No rule-based matching. No hardcoded keywords. Pure transformer inference.

🧪 Live Example

Input:

Patient is a 62-year-old male with chest pain, ST elevation, shortness of breath.
History of hypertension. ECG shows STEMI. Troponin elevated.

Summary:

Patient is a 62-year-old male with chest pain, ST elevation and shortness of breath.
ECG shows STEMI. Troponin is elevated. History of hypertension.

ICD-10 Suggestions:

1. Hypertension and high blood pressure (I10-I15)  —  93%
2. Stroke and cerebrovascular disease (I60-I69)    —  38%
3. Urinary tract infection and kidney disease      —  18%
